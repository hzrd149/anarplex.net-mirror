Register your nickname

Many features of the server can only be used with a registered nickname. To register your nickname, follow these two simple steps:

  1. Register your nick on irc: /msg nickserv register <password>
    Replace <password> with a secure password of your choosing.
  2. Confirm your nick via our website,
    Tor: http://y5fmhyqdr6r7ddws.onion/nickconfirm/confirm
    Clearnet https://anarplex.net/nickconfirm/confirm

Failing to register can result in not being able to join certain channels, not have private conversations with certain other users, or not even be able to use any service during times when we raise the defcon.
