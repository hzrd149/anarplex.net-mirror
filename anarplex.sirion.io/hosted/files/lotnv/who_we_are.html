Who We Are

The Trans-National Vanguard is a network of people with shared values like loyalty, independence, privacy and experimental living.

We are leading nomadic lives centered around our own cultural institutions, mediated through digital networks and settling where we are welcome to express how a livable future looks like.

When nation-states become obsolete and high systemic inter-dependence of failing structures threaten the life-style of a whole generation, we answer by living in resilient networks of people, goods and services. The future is here and we operate in it.
